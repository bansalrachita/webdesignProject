The Online Course Design Orientation Program is a 4-week, asynchronous, online experience designed to: familiarize faculty with teaching and learning in an online environment; link principles of learning to the online context; and empower faculty to work other professionals on the development of an online course. The course is organized into weekly sessions.

It is expected that each week participants will watch content modules and videos and complete a course assignment that applies the week’s content to the participant’s own course. We estimate the work to require approximately two hours per week to complete. To attain the full benefit of the experience, participants will complete assignments, review the work of others, and interact with peers in discussion.If you are interested in joining the Center For Advancing Teaching Learning Through Research (CALTR) online course development orientation program offered by Northeastern University, please Sign-Up below.
