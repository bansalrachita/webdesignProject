Dr. Lance Lewis has been with the Division of Online & Hybrid Studies since its inception in Feb 2014. He has been engaged with online learning at Northeastern since XXXX. He most recently served as Director of online programs for Direct Entry and Distance Learning for Bouve' college of health Sciences at Northeastern University.

He is also an associate member of the graduate faculty in the Northeastern Graduate School of Education, teaching face-to-face, hybrid and fully online courses beginning in 1993. At Northeastern, Dr. Lance directs Online, the centralized home for Northeastern University online degree programs using the course management system.
